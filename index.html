Thomas Kipf

PhD candidate

University of Amsterdam

I am a first year PhD student in Deep Learning for Network Analysis at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Prof. Max Welling. My main area of interest is large-scale inference for structured data and semi-supervised learning. I am also interested in self-organization and learning in biological systems.

My formal background is in Physics (M.Sc. hons. 2016, B.Sc. 2014 at FAU). During my studies, I have had exposure to a number of fields and eventually developed a deep interest for Machine Learning, following a short interlude in Neuroscience-related research at the Max Planck Institute for Brain Research in Frankfurt, Germany.
