Thomas Kipf

PhD candidate

University of Amsterdam

I am a first year PhD student in deep learning for network analysis at the University of Amsterdam, supervised by Prof. Max Welling. My main area of interest is large-scale inference for structured data and semi-supervised learning. I am also interested in self-organization and learning in biological systems.

Previously, I developed recurrent neural network-based approaches for the analysis of graph-structured data in connectomics at the Max Planck Institute for Brain Research, supervised by Dr. Moritz Helmstaedter. My formal background is in physics (M.Sc. hons. 2016, B.Sc. 2014 at FAU), where I have mainly focused on theoretical quantum optics.
